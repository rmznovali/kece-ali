Erdal Görgün

Artist Keçe Ali

Bio.

Erdal Görgün was born in Germany Stuttgart Heidenheim, 1982. He graduated from Dokuz Eylul Üniversily Fine Arts Education in 2003. As of 2019, there are 10 solo exhibitions and 23 mixed exhibitions. He participated in various workshops and he has awards on different categories and levels. Finally, he was the art director of ‘The Soul' film in Cannes 2014.

Erdal Görgün, the grandson of Keçeci Ali Efe, who took place in Kuvayi Milliye and fought on the Bademli Front, uses the signature of Keçe Ali in his paintings to commemorate his grandfather.
